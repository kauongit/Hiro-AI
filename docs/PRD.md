---

1. Executive Summary

HIRO is an AI-powered recruitment intelligence platform that helps recruiters discover, analyze, rank, and understand candidates beyond traditional Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS). Instead of relying solely on keyword matching, HIRO uses semantic AI, candidate intelligence, explainable AI, and multi-factor scoring to identify the most suitable candidates for a given role.

The platform aims to reduce hiring bias, save recruiter time, and improve hiring quality by providing transparent and intelligent candidate recommendations.

---

2. Problem Statement

Traditional recruitment systems rely heavily on keyword matching, resulting in:

- High-quality candidates being overlooked.
- Recruiters spending hours reviewing resumes manually.
- Limited understanding of candidate potential.
- Lack of transparency in AI recommendations.
- Poor matching between company requirements and candidate capabilities.

---

3. Solution

HIRO introduces an AI Recruitment Intelligence Platform that:

- Understands job descriptions semantically.
- Extracts meaningful candidate insights.
- Generates Candidate DNA profiles.
- Performs intelligent multi-factor ranking.
- Explains every hiring recommendation.
- Assists recruiters through an AI Copilot.

---

4. Objectives

- Reduce resume screening time.
- Improve candidate-job matching accuracy.
- Discover hidden talent.
- Increase recruiter productivity.
- Build trust with Explainable AI.
